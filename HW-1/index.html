Who Am I?

Hi! My name is Jo Curtis. I'll also respond to Jo Jo and other such variations of Jo. I am an artist, and a big fan of nosalgic feelings, shocker. I am a fanatic of anything 90's or retro-futursitic. The 90's and bright colors are my jimmy jam. I wear primarily black, but most of my art represnts my love for color and nostalgia. If you havent came to this conclusion yet, I am an avid lover of all things anime as well. I hope to one day make some sort of living from animation or motion design. Artistic and personal goals. I am in school for media arts, but my artsitic interest has many venues. I am an illustrator, painter, photographer, fashion designer, designer, and somewhat of a creative coder.
